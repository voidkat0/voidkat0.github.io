Chess & 5D Chess

I've been playing chess since middle school and learned many gambits and openers, however I like to mix my play style to use the logic of these well established moves without being very predictable when playing against someone who has an algorithmic playstyle, as I think in most cases adding calculated unpredictability against players who rely on these algorithms is the best strategy. I also feel that adaptibility and not relying very heavily on these algorithms myself keeps my skills sharp. However I could rarely find people to play against, so I started playing chess online, including 5D chess with multilinear timetravel (in which I am undefeated), which has recently become my favorite variant of chess because of the added challenge and need to keep track of so many variables
