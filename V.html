I have made ongoing contributions to...

Every Name Counts

I have done 22 transcriptions of Nazi germany historical documents concerning concentration camps, ranging from prisoner registration forms to personal effects cards, in order to help build a database of the names of the victim's relatives.

Research Project GloSAT

I have done 12 transcripts of captain's logbooks for a climate research project looking to map the global surface temperature in the early industrial era, in order to better understand how the climate has changed.

Wildlife Kenya Project

I've identified wildlife, species, number, and various other factors in 25 images for Project Wildlife Kenya. This conservation project aims to identify and track the giraffe population in Kenya along with other animals.

OceanEyes Project for PIFSC

I've identified the species of fish, along with the number, and direction they're swimming in 225 photos. This is all to help moniter the populations of the "deep 7" sea fish off the coast of Hawaii.

Research Project ACRE AR

I've done 51 transcriptions for project ACRE AR of Argentinan historical records, in order to recover the data and help climate scientist understand the evolution of the Southern Hemisphere's Climate
